ABOUT THE SHARED TASK

For worshop details please visit: http://lcs2.iiitd.edu.in/CONSTRAINT-2022/


  • Tasks- The CONSTRAINT-2022 shared task on the topic "Visual-semantic Role-labeling for Understanding Memes" invites participation in two subtasks:

    • Entity detection from memes: The first subtask focuses on detecting references being made within memes. There are various types of entities like a person, organization, community or geopolitical entity that are referred to or depicted within a meme via positive, negative, or neutral connotations. The objective of this subtask is to detect the set of valid entities either depicted or mentioned within a given meme.

    • Role labeling for memes: The second subtask emphasizes detecting which entities are glorified, vilified or victimized, within a meme. Assuming the frame of reference as the meme author's perspective, the objective is to classify for a given pair of a meme and an entity, whether the entity is being referenced as Hero vs. Villain vs. Victim vs. Other, within that meme.
